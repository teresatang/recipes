Ingredients:
 - cream cheese
 - cream of tartar
 - milk
 - 3 eggs
 - corn flour
 - sugar

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking
    pans.
 
 2. Separate eggs
 
 3. Beat cream cheese to smooth. Add milk, 1/2 sugar, egg york, corn flour and blend them together.
 
 4. Beat egg white. Add cream of tartar. 1/2 sugar. Beat until egg white stand.
 
 5. Gently mix 3 and 4 together. 
 
 6. Bake 30 to 35 minutes in a wide pan with boiling water. Cool completely before removing from oven.
